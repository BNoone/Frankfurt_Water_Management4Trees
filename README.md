**Frankfurt has 161,000 street trees. On a hot June day, a city gardener has limited water. Which trees do you water first?**

This tool combines tree biology (crown size, species, height), live weather (temperature, rain, humidity) and urban heat island data (taken from DWD as of May 2026 - one of the hottest months in recorded history) into a single priority score per tree — so the answer is one click away.
